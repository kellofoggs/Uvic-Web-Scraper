MUS201B - Language of Music

Description

A continuation of 201A. The structural principles, harmonic and contrapuntal practices of tonal music of the 19th century explored through analysis and composition.

Units

1.5

Hours: lecture-lab-tutorial

3-0-0

Pro Forma

No

Prerequisites

  • Complete all of:
    • MUS201A - Language of Music (1.5)
  • or permission of the school.

Course offered by

School of Music
